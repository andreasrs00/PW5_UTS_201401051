Grand Seiko SBGZ009

The movement was crafted by the Micro Artist Studio, the team behind the Masterpiece Collection, and features the Spring Drive Caliber 9R02, which has a maximum power reserve of approximately 84 hours when fully wound. This is made possible by the combination of the Dual Spring Barrel and Torque Return System. When the mainspring has been fully wound and the torque output is highest, approximately 30% of the power isn’t needed to maintain the watch’s precision. The ingenious integration of these mechanisms not only ensures an extended power reserve but also underscores Grand Seiko's commitment to pushing the boundaries of horological innovation. Exemplifying precision and innovation via the dual spring Barrel.
